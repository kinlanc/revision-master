📚 Revision Master: Your Personal Study Planner

Welcome to your Revision Master! This planner is designed to help you visualise your progress and stay on top of your revision schedule. Since this is a portable app, all your data is saved directly on your device - no accounts or passwords required.

🚀 Getting Started (Setup)

To begin, visit the live planner here: https://kinlanc.github.io/revision-master/

When you open the planner for the first time:

Enter Your Name: This will personalise your dashboard.

Select Your Curriculum: (e.g. IGCSE, IBDP, etc.).

Add Your Subjects: We’ve started you with a few, but you can add or remove as many as you like.

Click 'Build My Planner': Your dashboard will be generated instantly.

📋 Managing Your Topics

Each subject is shown on your dashboard.

Adding Topics: Click the 'Add Topic' button on a subject bar to add a specific unit or topics.

Editing Names: You can click directly on the name of any topic to rename it (e.g. change 'Introduction' to 'Photosynthesis').

Deleting: Hover over a row, then click the Trash Icon to remove a topic.

🚦 Tracking Your Progress

One of the important parts of revision is knowing where you stand. Use the dropdown menu next to each topic to set its status:

⚪ Not Started: You haven't looked at this yet.

🔴 Needs Focus: You’ve looked at it, but you don't understand it well yet.

🟠 Building: You’re starting to get it, but you need more practice.

🟢 Mastered: You are confident and ready for an exam on this topic.

🕒 The 'Last Reviewed' Feature

To beat the 'forgetting curve', you need to review topics multiple times and to ensure all those reds become green.

Every time you sit down and study a topic, click the 'Log Session' button.

The planner will automatically record today's date.

Aim: Try to ensure no topic has a 'Last Reviewed' date older than two weeks!

💾 Saving Your Progress

There are two ways to keep your data safe:

🌐 The Web Link (Recommended)

If you are using the official website link at https://kinlanc.github.io/revision-master/, the planner automatically saves your progress to your browser's 'Local Storage'. You can simply close the tab and return later; your data will still be there as long as you use the same browser on the same device.

📥 The 'Save Portable App' Button

If you want to move your data to a different computer, or if you are worried about clearing your browser history, click 'Save Portable App'.

This downloads a new file containing all your current data.

Pro Tip: Every time you download a new version, you should delete your old one to avoid confusion. Think of it like saving a game—you only need the most recent save file!

🖨️ Printing

If you want to show your progress to a teacher or stick a physical copy on your wall:

Click 'Print PDF' at the top.

The app will automatically hide all the buttons and menus, leaving you with a clean, professional-looking revision report.

Good luck with your revision!
